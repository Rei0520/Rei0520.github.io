The Y Campaign: Our Path to Complete Sustainability

Each passing day, climate change continues to become a more pressing issue for the Earth as we know it. The beautiful planet we live in is constantly threatened by the negative effects of human activity.

Therefore, as part of our initiative to be a responsible company, we at Reebok aim to create high-quality products that are environmentally friendly and safe for the planet.

























A Sustainable Future for All

As part of our drive to always go the extra mile, in the name of sustainability, we have evolved our process to be as eco-friendly as possible:

Our brand is committed to using more sustainable materials throughout our manufacturing process, and in our shops.
With the help of the industry, we have also made efforts towards dealing with the impact of microplastic materials.
Our company is also commited to recycling, giving new life to unwanted materials, for example by using recycled polyster and regenerated nylon.
With a Fashion Transparency Index score of 69%, Reebok is committed to disclosing our impact to the environment, and society at large.

New Shoes, Old Materials

In addition, to commemorate our move towards sustainable manufacturing, we released a collection of sustainable shoes, made with plant-based or recycled materials.

Our Sustainability Goals

30% Reduced greenhouse gases from suppliers
35% Reduced water usage per employee
100% Sustainable chemical use

In addition, we aim to be 100% carbon neutral by
2050.
