FlexWeave Collection
Introducing Flexweave shoe technology driven by technique. The powerful figure 8 design of Flexweave creates flexible support, lightweight durability and breathable strength. Flexweave from reebok is driving infinite potential in human movement.
Faster, Harder, Stronger
FlexWeave Technology harnesses the power of 8, weaving 8 fibers in a figure 8 pattern to create an upper that adapts and targets any part of the upper to deliver the necessary structure, strength, support and comfort. Experience the breakthrough weave that helps put more muscle into any fiber.
Woven Into Future
Changes in technique can produce inspiring results. Innovations like the forward pass, the Fosbury Flop, the slam dunk, and countless others have all changed the game completely. Thanks to insightful ideas about human movement.
